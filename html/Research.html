Research

First Research



Pill Navigation


A common layout pattern used in web development is the pill navigation, commonly referred to as a tabbed navigation. It is made up of several, usually horizontally aligned buttons that are used to move across a website's various pages.The pill navigation that was offered by w3school was discovered to be good after looking for a suitable navigation bar for the website. Consequently, w3school's pill navigation was used as a model.

Implementation:





Customizations were made to the W3Schools pill navigation after utilizing it as an example to better match the website's appearance and feel. A unique visual identity for the website that distinguishes it from others was developed by altering the colors and adding a logo.

Second Research



Product Page reference


For the making and choosing of product page design,This website was found and chosen for the website based on the product alignment. The product page is visually appealing and shows products in an attractive and ordered manner by utilizing this design as a model.

Implementation:





After selecting a website as a model for the product page design, some simple changes were done to better suit the requirements. We added add to cart button to make purchase easier in future, discounted price and added more button to get update on uncomming items.

Footer reference

Fourth Research

Fifth Research



Pop up Login Page reference


The " Pop-Up Apply form" form on the W3schools website caught my eye while I was looking for a design for the "Login" Form form for the website. Simple lines served as the input fields on the form, which had a minimalist appearance. This style has a clean, modern appearance and is visually appealing.

Implementation:





Used the simple " Pop-Up Apply form" form W3schools as a model for the "Login Form" page and made adjustments such adding radio buttons and connections to the social media accounts. These extra components allowed us to provide the visitors more alternatives and ways to interact.
