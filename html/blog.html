TECHNOLOGY

Technology refers to the knowledge and skills that are acquired and applied to design and develop items or products for use in real-world situations. Additionally, it simplifies and relieves stress of our daily life. In the contemporary world, technology is the most important key to both science and engineering as well as to everyday living. Its primary objective is to simplify and alleviate the complexities of daily life. Due to different invention and advancements of Technologies our society have changed a lot. In addition to advancing science, technology also advances economic growth and increases human prosperity. However, it is essential to recognize that technology's impact is twofold, yielding both positive and negative consequences that cannot be disregarded.

POSITIVE IMPACTS OF TECHNOLOGY

Technology is a valuable tool with numerous positive impacts. It simplifies global communication, allowing us to connect with diverse individuals worldwide. It enhances efficiency in our daily lives and education. For example, students can now catch up on missed classes or assignments through online courses. Transportation has also improved, making it easier to travel comfortably from one place to another. Online banking has made financial transactions more secure and convenient. Technology spreads awareness quickly and improves our day-to-day lives, making tasks easier and faster. However, it's important to acknowledge that technology also has negative effects that should not be ignored.
