SONY ALPHA 1


Sony Alpha 1 (a1) is a high-end full-frame mirrorless camera with a 50MP Stacked BSI-CMOS sensorand in-body image stabilization.

The a1 offers blackout-free shooting on its giant 9.44M-dot (240 fps) EVF at burst rates of up to 30 fps.

It has Sony's latest autofocus system, with fast processing and face/eye/animal detection.

The electronic shutter is designed to eliminate flicker and offers a flash sync of 1/200 sec.
(it's an impressive 1/400 sec with the mechanical shutter).
